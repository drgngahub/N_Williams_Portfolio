These are a few artists and creators who inspire me

Beeple (Mike Winkelmann)

His futuristic digital artworks mix dystopian themes with pop culture, showing what’s possible when daily discipline meets creative vision. I’m drawn to how wild and intricate his work is.

Ash Thorp

His cinematic, cyberpunk-inspired art blends digital abstraction with storytelling. His visuals feel like dreams carved out of code and light — and that inspires me to experiment more.

Timothy J. Reynolds

He creates stylized, low-poly 3D art that feels both playful and polished. I love how his work makes digital landscapes feel tangible and peaceful.
