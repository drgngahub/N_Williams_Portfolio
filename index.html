I’m Nate, a student at San Francisco State University studying Visual Communication Design.
I’m originally from the Bay Area and a U.S. Navy veteran.
This site is my first attempt at building something from scratch with code, and it represents a big step forward in learning how to express myself as both a designer and a developer.
I'm still figuring out what kind of creative work I want to focus on, but I’m starting to enjoy the process of experimenting, trying new tools, and getting things wrong before I get them right.
Web design feels a little intimidating at times, but it’s also satisfying when things finally click.
My background gives me a different perspective on problem solving, structure, and communication.
I’m interested in how visual language can shape experience, and I’m curious about where that interest might take me.
This project is just the beginning, and I’m looking forward to improving over time.
